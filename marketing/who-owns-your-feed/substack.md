# Your Feed Has a Checkbook

The three platforms that touch your screen before breakfast all run US PACs, and all three lean the same way. The gap is the story: the biggest spender outspends the smallest roughly 100-to-1 — and the smallest spender answers to a parent company that isn't American. Can you match the app to the cash?

Each puzzle shows you real federal campaign contribution data from OpenSecrets.org — party split, dollar amounts, everything sourced and linked. Tap a company, tap the chart you think is theirs, and find out if you're right. Get all three and every source link opens up.

No login. No paywall. No app. Just facts, sourced.
