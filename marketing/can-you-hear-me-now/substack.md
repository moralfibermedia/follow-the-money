# Your Phone Bill Funds Somebody

You pay it every month without thinking: the phone bill. AT&T, Verizon, and T-Mobile sit behind it — and in the 2024 cycle, one of the three gave exactly $0 to federal candidates. The other two look almost identical on paper. Almost. Silence, it turns out, is also a data point.

Each puzzle shows you real federal campaign contribution data from OpenSecrets.org — party split, dollar amounts, everything sourced and linked. Tap a company, tap the chart you think is theirs, and find out if you're right. Get all three and every source link opens up.

No login. No paywall. No app. Just facts, sourced.
