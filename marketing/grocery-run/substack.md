# The Most Political Thing in Your Cart Is the Receipt

Groceries are the biggest weekly purchase most households make — and Walmart, Kroger, and Costco all convert a slice of that into political money. Here's the puzzle: one of the three splits its federal donations almost exactly down the middle. The other two pick a side. Can you spot the nearly-purple one?

Each puzzle shows you real federal campaign contribution data from OpenSecrets.org — party split, dollar amounts, everything sourced and linked. Tap a company, tap the chart you think is theirs, and find out if you're right. Get all three and every source link opens up.

No login. No paywall. No app. Just facts, sourced.
