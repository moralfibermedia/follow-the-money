# Premium Groceries, Premium Politics

The premium-grocery aisle has its own politics. Whole Foods answers to Amazon now, Trader Joe's is privately held, and Publix is employee-owned and Florida-grown. Two of the three lean the same direction — but one of those two spends thirty-one times more than the other. Does the donation profile look like a grocery store, or like its owner?

Each puzzle shows you real federal campaign contribution data from OpenSecrets.org — party split, dollar amounts, everything sourced and linked. Tap a company, tap the chart you think is theirs, and find out if you're right. Get all three and every source link opens up.

No login. No paywall. No app. Just facts, sourced.
