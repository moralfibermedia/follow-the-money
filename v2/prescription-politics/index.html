Prescription Politics

Three pharma giants. Three donation profiles. Can you match the company to the cash?
OpenSecrets.org · 2024 cycle · federal candidates  ·  3 companies · ~2 min

Pfizer, Johnson & Johnson, Eli Lilly — three names on the pharmacy shelf, three political action committees writing checks in Washington. Big Pharma hedges both sides. Not equally.

The numbers below are federal campaign contributions for the 2024 cycle, as tracked by OpenSecrets.org: corporate PAC giving plus employees' individual donations to federal candidates. They are not direct corporate treasury gifts — those are illegal — and they don't include lobbying, which is a separate (and much larger) ledger.

You'll see three unlabeled donation profiles — party split and total spend. One hint before you start: only one of these three PACs tilts toward the party that votes against letting Medicare negotiate drug prices.
