## Introducing: Follow the Money

We built something. It's free, it takes sixty seconds, and you'll walk away knowing something you didn't know before about the companies you give money to every week.

**Three companies. Three donation profiles. Match the company to the cash.**

Each puzzle shows you real federal campaign contribution data from OpenSecrets.org — party split, dollar amounts, everything sourced and linked. You tap a company, tap the chart you think is theirs, and find out if you're right. Get all three correct and you can dive deeper into every source.

No login. No paywall. No app to download. Just facts.
