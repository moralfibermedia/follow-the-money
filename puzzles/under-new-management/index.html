Media Consolidation · 2025–26
CBS sold. MSNBC renamed. TikTok under new owners. Warner Bros. on the block. Can you put the takeover wave in order?
Sources: SEC filings · company announcements · court record
How to play: Put the five events in the order they happened — tap two cards to swap them, or use the arrows. Lock it in to reveal the dates.
In under a year, CBS changed hands, MSNBC lost its name, TikTok's U.S. operation got new part-owners, and the company behind HBO and CNN agreed to a $110.9 billion sale — to the same family that bought CBS. The order is the story. Can you rebuild it?
