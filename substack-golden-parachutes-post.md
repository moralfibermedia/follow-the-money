# The Biggest Company Doesn't Pay the Biggest CEO

Microsoft, Apple, Amazon, Google. Four companies you touch before breakfast, run by four of the most powerful executives alive. Here's a question that sounds simple and isn't: **rank them by what they take home.**

Go on — put Satya Nadella, Tim Cook, Andy Jassy, and Sundar Pichai in order, highest pay to lowest.

Most people get it wrong, because the ranking doesn't follow the logos. The biggest company doesn't cut the biggest check. And one of these four earns **less than a tenth** of another — for running a trillion-dollar business.

We didn't guess. Every figure comes straight from each company's SEC proxy filing — the official, to-the-dollar disclosure public companies are legally required to publish.
